Designing Tips

  • Match Colors. Blue wheels and a blue boost will match nicely with a blue car.
  • Light and Dark. A dark primary color used on a lightly colored decal adds emphasis to the decal. This makes it appealing and stands out more.
  • Black Wheels. Black wheels match with any color, allowing you to make the car's color anything you want and it still looks good
  • Anything. Do whatever you want to the car. The great thing about Rocket League is that there are so many options. You can customize your car however you like!
